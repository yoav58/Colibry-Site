About Us

The idea of Colibry was born a few years ago when the challenges of COVID-19 changed the rules of consumption forever.

This realization highlighted that the global market, and especially the Israeli market, was ready for the next phase in the shopping and trading era.

To meet this demand, we founded Colibry, a unique company specializing in logistics. Our mission is to help customers maximize their profits by managing all aspects of delivering goods to end customers, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.

We strive to shape the future of the industry by growing and expanding. With our one-of-a-kind dashboard, market-specific solutions, extensive network of partners, and AI-driven optimizations, we guarantee a smooth and efficient process for both our clients and their customers.
